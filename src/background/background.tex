Some underlying principles of the theoretical topics on which the  for this thesis are presented in this chapter.
All the topics of this chapter are crucial parts of the work but some of the material is presented in greater detail that is completely necessary for understanding the model.

This section is divided to three independent parts.
The first part covers the principles and handling of spatio-temporal data with emphasis on modelling it through the advection-diffusion model.
Subsequently, an introduction to neural networks theory is presented.
And finally, the concepts of Bayesian filtering are presented and the Kalman filter is formulated and extended to an ensemble based version. 

By reviewing these concepts, this chapter aims to give a theoretical foundation to understand the proposed model's underlying mechanisms presented in subsequent sections.
